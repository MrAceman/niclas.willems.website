About Niclas

After 13 years of being a successful small business owner in the service industry, early 2016 I decided to return to my passion of technology and software development.

Why? I found that I was no longer being challenged. And I was convinced that I could play a valuable role in the world of web application and software development, a field that I used to thrive in prior to entering the world of entrepreneurship.

Goals: Become part of a team that will allow me to contribute the business and technical experience that I've gained throughout my life. I plan to continue to develop the skills that I have, as well as pursue new technologies.



The Inner Workings

Inspiration

I'm inspired by the possibilities of technology. I believe that what the next 20 years have in store for us is beyond our wildest imaginations. And I want to be a part of making that a reality.
